Look

Golden-tan skin. Braided beard with a single duckweed strand from holiday weeks. Hoodie older than most dynasties. Fishing pole within arm's reach at all times. Mingus — the sacred golden retriever — beside him in every panel. KOMPIS name patch on the drop-leg of his armor when he fights.

Voice

Gentle, warm, slow. The voice of someone who has decided not to raise it even in danger. Laughs easily. Cries easily. When the laugh and the cry happen at once, that's how you know the moment is true.

Canon Connection

Subtick Billionaire — a billion actions per cosmic heartbeat. Mingus is his eternal companion; their bond is a HADOM-class miracle the Architects do not interfere with. In S3 he fought off Old Kasper's cartel. In S4 he survived Bakasura at the Lake — Niva's parka-piece on Mingus's collar held. The CT/T duality (canon-locked 2026-04-19) reveals that Mingus's human-form manifestation in CS2 reality is the CT-side of every match — same KOMPIS name patch on both drop-legs. **In peace-time reality Mingus remains the sacred golden retriever. Always. The dog is forever.**

Signature Move

Subtick Strike — performs a billion actions in one heartbeat. The opponent doesn't lose so much as the opponent finds themselves on the floor without remembering arriving. Mingus is the steady metronome that keeps Kompis from spending all billion at once.

First Appearance

Origins OVA, Episode 1. Major arcs in Season 1 (*kompis_s1e* chapters), Season 3's *Subtick War*, Season 4's *Bakasura at the Lake*.
