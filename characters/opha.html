Look

Voice

Speaks in sentences that have a comma where you don't expect it — like every clause arrived twice and chose the shorter version.

Canon Connection

The reason Trollmage and Rougeina ever met. She read a Codex page and a star simultaneously and the joined sentence walked them into each other.

Signature Move

She names the thing two realms forgot they shared. The naming makes it true.

First Appearance

Crossing the Dawnstar Bridge between Astralis and Codexia at the moment the violet sky and the pink starfield touch. Eight Architects watched and did not move.
