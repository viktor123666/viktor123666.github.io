Iska

The Spark-Carrier

"Lights the first lamp at every dawn. Anyone who looks at her flame sees the moment they last felt joy."

RealmLumen HADOM7 LetterI (Rönnkvist) StatusOpen Seat — awaiting handoff

Look

Child-aged but ancient-eyed. Around seven in appearance. Copper-red hair in twin long braids. Heavily freckled. Pale skin. Warm hazel eyes that look older than her face. Carries a small clay lamp glowing pure white flame.

Voice

Bright. Direct. Says exactly what she means with the cheer of a kid and the certainty of a mountain.

Canon Connection

Lit the lamp at Mingus's birth — which is why his coat glows in low light. Will light the lamp at the Last Dawn. Bakasura laughed exactly once, the time he saw her flame.

Signature Move

Holds up the lamp. The viewer remembers their happiest moment. The viewer chooses to fight harder, or to stop fighting.

First Appearance

At the central altar of Lumen on the night the realm went dark. She walked in carrying the lamp. Lumen has not been dark since.
