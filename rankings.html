HADOM Rankings

HADOM = hjärtslag, the Swedish word for heartbeat. The tick-rate of reality itself — how many actions a being can resolve within one human heartbeat. A normal human is HADOM 1. Scalelist champions reach into the millions. Vidar speaks once a century. Mingus is uncountable.
