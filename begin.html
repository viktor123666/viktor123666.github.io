It begins with a name.

The Author's true name — Viktor Rönnkvist — is the cosmic cipher. Fifteen letters, fifteen souls, and the Author himself as the sixteenth. Nothing else in fiction is built this way: the world IS the man, spelled out.

Five faces. One soul.

Vigolas, Kompis, Smurjan, Rougeina, Trollmage — they look like five champions. They are five fragments of one source-soul, wearing different faces. The Exodia Principle: without one part, the whole cannot be summoned. They are Vigge, distributed.
