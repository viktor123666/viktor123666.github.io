Continents · 3 regions

The Absence

Nothing. No light. No air. No ground. You have a body here only because the universe is being polite.

The whole of Null is one continent because it is nothing. Subdividing it would require something to subdivide.

Ferdinand's Roots

Silver-barked roots descending impossibly far through the absence.

The only 'continent' in Null that has structure — Ferdinand's silver roots. They are not Null's; they are Ferdinand's, but they are Null's only furniture.

The Inscriptions

Non-spatial region where the Seven Sin Codices were etched on Null's 'inside.'

The theoretical continent of Null. Where the pre-written things exist. Non-spatial. Non-temporal. Real.
