Continents · 4 regions

Bali's Garden

Terraced flower-gardens on a green-gold hillside. Always late morning.

The calm continent. Bali's domain. The only place in Trinity where Kali's fire cannot light.

Kali's Burn

Volcanic plateau. Black obsidian fields, perpetual dusk, red sky.

The fire continent. Kali's domain. The only place in Trinity where Bali's calm cannot settle.

The Fused Court

A circular plateau between the Garden and the Burn. Always dusk. Stone, silver, still water.

The balance continent. Where Smurjan's Fused voice holds the council. The realm's political and ritual heart.

The Echo Marches

Grey borderlands between Trinity and Echo realm. Mist. Silence. Mirrors embedded in stone.

The disputed edge. Where Echo's lobby for admission is carried out peacefully but endlessly. The Rejected live here as both buffer and witness.

Persona: Fierce. Loyal. Rarely laugh but laugh from the chest when they do.

on kalis_burn

Look: Veiled in black obsidian-thread. Hair cropped short. One silver piercing per loss.

Persona: Grieving. Dignified. Strong. Their grief is not weakness — it is Kali's truest expression.

on fused_court

Look: Robed in grey-silver with three braids — one black (Bali), one red (Kali), one white (Fused). Voice shifts between three tones mid-sentence.

Persona: Balanced. Careful. Impossible to pin to one opinion — which is the point.

The Pulse-Dancers

on fused_court

Look: Sleek. Muscled. Ankle-bells in trios. Wear form-fitting triple-colored garments (black/red/white).

Persona: Disciplined. Warm. The realm's heart-keepers. Without them, the Trinity's balance collapses.

The Rejected

on echo_marches

Look: Mixed origins — travelers from other realms who asked to join the Trinity and were politely refused. Carry one gift each from Smurjan as a parting mercy.

Persona: Weary. Generous. Often the kindest tribe in the realm. Their rejection has taught them, not hardened them.

The Mirror-Wardens

on echo_marches

Look: Half-masked silver. Robes stitched with tiny cracked mirror-fragments. One ear covered at all times — Echo whispers into open ears.

Persona: Watchful. Stoic. Slightly paranoid — but justly so.
