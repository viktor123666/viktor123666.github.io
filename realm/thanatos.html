Continents · 4 regions

The Outer Spiral

Curved plains of pale stone, etched with names. Mist. Soft wind.

The outermost ring of the spiral graveyard. Where the ancient dead rest. The Name-Carvers work here.

The Middle Ring

Cypress and stone. Tall memorial pillars. Silver mist at ankle-height.

The middle continent. Where names are sung, rung, and written into final form. The realm's working heart.

Fenna's Center

A circular sanctum at the spiral's heart. Marble floor. Single pillar. Single desk.

The innermost center. Fenna's domain. Where the universe's deaths are read, acknowledged, and passed on.

The Name-Snow Marshes

Wetland plains where falling snow is actually drifting carved names.

The southern marshes where the realm's name-snow falls thickest. The Snow-Gatherers are the realm's archivists-on-foot.

Tribes & Peoples · 8 groups

The Name-Carvers

on outer_spiral

Look: Tall, thin, ink-stained sleeves. Calluses on both hands from chisel work. Wear a small silver letter on a chain — their own name, carved by a predecessor.

Persona: Quiet. Precise. Respected. Their work is the realm's memory.

The Silent-Grievers

on outer_spiral

Look: Grey or black. Veiled. Walk the Memorial Walk daily. Hands ink-black from rubbing the stone to feel the names.

Persona: Meditative. Kind to visitors. Every Silent-Griever carries a grief they have chosen to represent the realm.

The Bell-Ringers

on middle_ring

Look: Strong-armed, bronze-gloved. Dark uniforms. Hair cropped. One bell-medal per year of service.

Persona: Earnest. Steadfast. Hate laziness. Take drinks after shift and sing old Thanatos hymns.

The Grief-Poets

on middle_ring

Look: Shawl-wrapped. Quill at the ear. Ink-stained sleeves and cheeks. Soft-eyed.

Persona: Gentle. Observant. Write the realm's uncatalogued tenderness.

The Ledger-Keepers (Death branch)

on fennas_center

Look: Pale, linen-robed, carry a small book always. Eyes soft but unfocused (from proximity to the Central Pillar).

Persona: Devoted. Quietly joyful. Grateful to serve a post that the universe depends on.

Fenna (Herself)

on fennas_center

Look: Tall, pale, long black hair braided in a single thick plait. Black robe with silver trim. Hands ink-free — she reads, not writes.

Persona: Kind in a way that is hard to notice. Will greet you in a language you have not yet learned but understand anyway. Has never been surprised.

The Snow-Gatherers

on the_name_snow_marshes

Look: Insulated hooded cloaks. Snowshoes. Net-gloves for catching drifting names. Pink-cheeked from wind.

Persona: Hardy. Cheerful. Tell tall tales around the Chapel fire. Respected by all other Thanatos tribes.

The Thirteenth-Bell Watchers

on the_name_snow_marshes

Look: Grey-cloaked, seven-striped sleeves (one stripe per year of service). Silver ear-cuff. Solemn.

Persona: Silent. Grave. Chosen by Fenna herself. It is the hardest post in the realm.
