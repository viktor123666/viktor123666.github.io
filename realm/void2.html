Continents · 3 regions

The Ring of Seats

A white marble plateau in perfect circle. 12 stone seats at the perimeter. The Seal at center.

The entire ring. The seats, the Seal, and the space between. VOID-II has no other geography.

The Library Beyond

A corridor extending infinitely in all six cardinal directions from the Ring.

Where the Architects' work is stored. Infinite in every direction. The Ledger Librarians tend it in silent shifts.

The Butterfly Garden

A small walled courtyard off the Ring. Pale gravel. Seven stone benches. White butterflies.

The realm's only soft place. Where decisions about to be made somewhere in the multiverse land as butterflies. Watched closely.
