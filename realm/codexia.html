Continents · 4 regions

The Spire Highlands

Seven colossal violet stone spires rising from misty moorlands.

The seven spires of Codexia — one per Sin Codex. Each spire is a library, a prison, and a patient teacher.

The Backwards Bayou

Swampland where water flows uphill and trees grow roots skyward.

The marshland where magic works backwards. Every incantation cast here must be inverted before speaking. Dangerous for anyone who cannot think in reverse.

The Necromancer Reach

Cold pine forest, fogged graveyards, black-ice rivers.

The eastern continent. Necromancers, stonemasons, and students who find corpses more polite than the living.

The Dancehall Basin

A basin of warm grasslands below the spires. One dancehall. That's it.

The one warm and uncomplicated place in Codexia. Even Trollmage visits when the reading becomes too heavy.
