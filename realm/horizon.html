Continents · 3 regions

The Platform

A vast stone platform stretching to the horizon. No walls. No roof. Perpetual dawn.

The realm's main continent. Where souls wait between deaths and rebirths. K-II walks it slowly.

The Dawn Coast

A thin coast beyond the Platform's edge. Horizon Sea below. Permanent sunrise above.

The coastal fringe. Where the Dawn Sparrow handlers and the realm's few fishers work. Gentle. Warm.

The Silent Fields

Grassy plains between the Platform and the Coast. Single-color horizon-gold grass.

The quiet in-between. Between the Platform and the Coast. The realm's meadow.
