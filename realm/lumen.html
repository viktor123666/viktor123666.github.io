Continents · 3 regions

The Center

A single point of white-gold light, expanding outward in perfect concentric waves.

The Center is the entire realm's reason. The one place in the cosmos where every heartbeat is born.

The First Ring

The outermost confirmed perimeter of Lumen. Soft glow. Solid ground rare.

The Ring is the realm's outer edge. Also theoretical — nobody has measured it.

The Outer Silence

Beyond the First Ring. Speculative. Nothing observable.

Theoretical continent. Beyond the edge. No confirmed inhabitants, places, or properties. Only the Architects may speak of it — and they rarely do.
