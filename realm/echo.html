Continents · 3 regions

The Mirror Hall (Vast)

An infinite mirrored corridor. No doors. No ceiling. Floor is also a mirror.

The Hall is the entire realm — mostly. It extends in all directions. The Half-Selves wander it.

The Reflection Sea

A motionless 'sea' of pure silver — a horizontal mirror stretching to the horizon.

The Sea is Echo's only liquid feature. Divers use it to glimpse other realms as reflections. Dangerous — stay too long and your reflection becomes the diver.

The Yearning Edge

The rim of the Hall where Echo presses against the Trinity border nightly.

The realm's political boundary. The only 'active' continent — where Echo is not merely reflective but ambitious.
