The Lore of HADOM

In the Scalelist Universe, power is not measured by strength or speed alone. It is measured by HADOM — how many actions a being can execute within a single heartbeat.

The heart is the clock of existence. Hjärtslag. Every beat is a tick of reality. A normal human manages one action per beat. The Scalelist champions execute thousands — millions — in that same instant.

But there is a paradox: when your heart races with fear, you gain more ticks — but each tick loses resolution. Precision fades. The calm warrior, with a slow steady heart, executes fewer ticks but with perfect quantum resolution.

This is why Vigolas, who has mastered his heart for 50,000 years, operates at ∞-1. Not through speed. Through absolute stillness.
